Know Your Doctor

Meet Dr. Arvind Sharma

Dedicated to bringing international-standard cardiology treatments directly to rural communities.

Dr. Arvind Sharma Founder & Lead Cardiologist

"My mission is to ensure that no heart in our villages suffers due to lack of timely, high-quality medical guidance."

Dr. Arvind Sharma is a senior cardiologist with over 18 years of experience. After practicing in premier hospitals across metro cities, he decided to open Spandan Heart Clinic to serve people in rural districts and villages who often have to travel hundreds of kilometers for simple cardiac checkups.

Qualifications MBBS, MD (Medicine), DM (Cardiology)
Specializations Heart Attacks, High BP, Blockage, Echo, ECG
Experience 18+ Years (Former Senior Consultant in City Hospitals)
Certifications Fellow of Cardiological Society of India (CSI)
Our Medical Mission:

To prevent sudden heart failures in rural populations through early screening, affordable tests, correct medication guidance, and patient-first compassionate education.
